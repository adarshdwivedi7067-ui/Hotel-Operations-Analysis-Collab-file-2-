**Developed by:** Adarsh Dwivedi | **Role:** Data & Business Analyst  

---

##  Project Overview  
Elite Hotels International face challenges in managing booking patterns and customer satisfaction.  
This project analyzes a comprehensive dataset of hotel bookings to uncover trends in guest behavior and operational efficiency.  
Using **Python (Jupyter Notebook)**, raw booking data is transformed into actionable insights to help management optimize occupancy and reduce cancellations.  

---

##  Business Problem  
Understanding booking dynamics is crucial for hospitality success. This project addresses the following core business questions:  

- **Booking Trends:** How do booking patterns differ between City Hotels and Resort Hotels?  
- **Cancellation Drivers:** What factors (lead time, deposit type, etc.) contribute most to cancellations?  
- **Revenue Optimization:** How does the Average Daily Rate (ADR) vary across market segments and seasons?  
- **Guest Profiling:** What are the preferences of repeated guests vs. new customers?

##  Key Insights  
- **Cancellation Correlation:** High lead time strongly predicts cancellations; advance bookings without deposits are most at risk  
- **Market Segmentation:** Online TA (Travel Agent) drives highest bookings but also higher cancellations vs. Direct bookings  
- **Seasonal Demand:** Revenue peaks in summer months; City Hotels maintain consistent occupancy year‑round  
- **Guest Loyalty:** Repeated guests cancel less, proving loyalty programs are vital for stability  

---

##  Strategic Recommendations  
- **Operations:** Implement stricter cancellation policies or non‑refundable deposits for high lead‑time bookings  
- **Marketing:** Focus promotional offers on Direct booking channels to increase ADR and reduce third‑party commissions  
- **Revenue Management:** Apply dynamic pricing during peak seasons (July/August) to maximize revenue  
- **Guest Experience:** Personalize services for repeated guests to improve retention
